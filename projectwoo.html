2 years ago, I left the country to embark on a Volunteer project with Surf For Life in Las Salinas, Nicaragua. After my experience in Nicaragua, I promised myself I would work hard in order to be able to support this non-profit financially.

In order to accomplish I reached out to Project WOO to see where I could help out. I had a quick response back and was asked to commit to raising funds for the medical supplies that they need to keep this "project" running smoothly.

In 2016, my goal is to raise $1500+ dollars to support Project WOO with enough medical supplies for their Community Health Center for the entire year! That right, for the year!

Join me in creating change and sustain this village by maintaining its health.

Thank you very much for your support!
